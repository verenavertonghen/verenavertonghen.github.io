About me

Design & Coding

In 2013 I became interested in learning how to build websites. I studied web design and user experience, learning how to code and create webpages. In my work, I often had many responsibilities, including designing and developing website interfaces. I always tried to improve how webpages looked and worked together, which led me to develop an appreciation for design systems that make websites more consistent and useful. As a designer, I aim to create the best experience for users by thinking about what they need and want and trying to see things from their perspective. In my free time, I enjoy baking, drawing, playing padel, and walking my dog.

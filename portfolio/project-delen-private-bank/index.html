Delen Private Bank - App for clients

During my time at Delen Private Bank I worked on the app for clients.

Client

Delen Private Bank

Skills used

UX, UI, Frontend Development

Date

October 10, 2017

Challenge

Design and develop applications by a high standard because they are being used by clients. An important requirement was that the app should be easy to use for older customers.

Solution

I contributed to the design of several features in the app. Other notable achievements include the addition of graphic illustrations, more design consistency and more accessible colors.

Responsibilities and achievements

  • Create low fidelity wireframes in the early stages of the design.
  • Create high fidelity mockups with the existing styleguide/design system.
  • Create clickable prototypes to demonstrate the full product experience.
  • Create new UI components when needed that would be added to the design system.
  • Present designs to business in all phases of the design process to gather feedback and adapt to the needs of the users.
  • Gather requirements in collaboration with business analysts.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure the feasability of the design and to give feedback after implemenation.
  • Present and lead the design meeting.
  • Present achieved work and designs during the sprint review meetings.
  • Organise user testing sessions and gather insights.
  • Organise and lead design thinking workshops with the business
  • Lead organizer of the first Delen Hackathon to attract new young talent to Delen.
  • Served as a mentor to an intern, providing guidance and support throughout their internship.
  • Facilitated sprint planning meetings with cross-functional teams to prioritize tasks, estimate effort, and set achievable sprint goals.
  • Organise team events.
  • Onboard new designers.
